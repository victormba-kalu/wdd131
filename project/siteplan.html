Princess

Site Purpose

The 'Princess' website aims to be a digital storytelling platform sharing the inspiring mission and founding story of the 'Princess: Connecting Care' initiative. Its primary goal is to build foundational engagement by deeply resonating with visitors and emphasizing our commitment to vulnerable mothers and their children, subtly introducing the types of resources we aspire to connect, and providing clear channels for initial contact

Scenerios

Question: "I'm a new mom, and I feel so incredibly lost and overwhelmed. My baby needs food, and I just don't know where to turn. Is there anyone out there who understands and can offer immediate help, even just someone to listen or point me to basic necessities?"
Question: "I want to make a real, tangible difference for mothers and babies who are struggling. The 'Princess' initiative caught my eye - what's the heartfelt story behind it, and how can I be certain my contribution, whether it's my time or resources, will truly create a positivechange?"

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Rose (#C75B91)
Usage: Accents (buttons, hover states), subheadings, subtle background tints for sections, illustrations/icons
Secondary Color: Royal Grape (#6A4DBA)
Usage: Main header background, primary page headings, borders/dividers, form elements, secondary buttons.
Accent 1: Very Light Gray (#F0F2F5)
Usage: Overall background of the site and content areas.
Accent 2: Charcoal (#1F2937)
Usage: for the primary body text and footer background.

Typography

Primary Font: Inter
Type: Sans-serif
Usage: It is applied to all headings, body text, navigation elements, buttons, forms, and any other accents or labels across the entire site. Different weights of Inter are used to create visual hierarchy.

Wireframe

The wireframe for the 'Princess' website is designed to be clean, intuitive, and user-friendly. It features a simple layout with a header containing the logo and navigation links, a main content area for storytelling and resource introduction, and a footer with contact information and social media links.
